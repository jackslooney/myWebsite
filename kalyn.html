    • Background

    Found rifling through the garbage in Lonelywood, Zohe is a rather difficult character. Not much is known about her background, her memory is rather hazy, with her first memory being given to a rather large creature and being bombarded by a strange group afterwards. As Zohe doesn’t know where to go if she were to leave this group, she has chosen to stick with them for the element of safety.

    Stats

    Zohe herself is a rather…interesting specimen. Standing at two foot three, she is a Rouge Faerie Elf. She is proficient in athletics, sleight of hand, insight, and intimidation, and extra proficient in perception and deception. She does, however, lack any sort of strength.

    Fighting

    Zohe’s main advantage in combat is her sneak attack. She is unable to be surprised, therefore other creatures cannot get advantage. She also holds the Seelie’s Cort Jewel, which is a rapier that can take the form of a piece of jewelry. It changes back when she speaks her command word. She is able to use it to perform certain spells:

    • Disguise Self
    • Compelled Duel
    • Faerie Fire
    • Misty Step
