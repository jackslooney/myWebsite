    Bryn Shander

    Our history of betting

    When entering Bryn Shander, our party was met with a young artificier, He decided to try and take one of our party members, which we tried to stop. This then leads to a bet, if we won we got to keep our party member.
    Unforunatly, we would also be losing a differnt party member if we one. The bet was a contest of strength so Ookla was the one to participate. The contest was who could through Seran the farthest. Ookla channeled all his energy and threw Seran a whopping 80 ft across the city. Long story short Ookla won the bet and Seran was given over to Matty to experiment on. These experiments would eventually lead to the lose of Seran's arm.


    Our second Bet

    Upon returning to the city we waged another bet, this time between two party members. The goal was for one to attack the other and still be able to stand after the attack. Ookla in his infinite wisdom gathered a crown of people and started taking bets on who would win. Ignom uses his most powerful attack at Stadler and fires. Stadler being able to change his form at will was able to absorb the attack and thus remained standing. This exchange brought in quite a bit of capitol due to the amount of people betting. The amount of money aquired are as such:

    1. Ookla: MUCH
    2. Ivory: Not as much
    3. Seran: 10 gold (from stealing)
    4. Stadler: 0 gold
    5. Ignom: 0 gold

    Starting Our Journey

    Our individual party members found themselves trapped in a frozen wasteland due to various cercumstances. The party started by meeting in a little town known as Bremen, and shortly after meeting, the party was given a glorified fetch quest to go catch what is known as a Chwinga. During this quest, the party had to cross multiple frozen rivers, which resulted in most of the party being dropped into the icy waters.This is where our parties fear of rivers began. After this quest was completed, and a small amount of money gained, the party set off together in search of more adventure...

    A Summary of Events Leading to Present Time

    The party found themselves in Targos (the only town that connects to Bremen by road), and ended up offering to help a townsperson in need. This man's husband had gone off on an expidition to a local mountain range called Kelvin's Cairn, and hadn't returned for some time. The party offered to find him, for almost no cost whatsoever. During this rescue mission, the party met multiple notable people, including an accomplished (and a little crazy) arficier named Matty. Matty unethically kidnaps and experiments on Seran, then offers Ivory an apprenticeship.
    A notable quote from Matty:

    I'm just gunna take him for my experiments, if you try to stop me, I kill you.
    - Matty

    From here the party:

    • Explores further East
    • Eats some incredible chowder
    • Rescues, and returns the husband

    After this, the party gains items, strange attributes, and other abilities. Matty further experiements on Seran, and turns him into an undead vampire. This creates Some problems. In Easthaven, the half the party accidentally assaults a doctor, and then convinces him it didn't happen, while the other half committs murder most foul. It can be assumed that the party would rather not go back to Easthaven after such crimes.

    The party then heads to Lonelywood, the home of Ignum, who lives with his grandmother. His grandmother turns out to be a demon, and kills basically everyone in the town, as well as a member of the party, Ivory. Distrought, Ookla walks multiple days and nights to Bryn Shander to bring Ivory to Matty, in hopes he can be resurrected. Matty extracts Ivory's soul and places it into a metal housing, essentially turning Ivory from Elvish, to Warforged. As payment, Matty charges the party with bringing down a corrupt, evil city of artificiers, Argona. The party agrees, without knowing what they are getting into. Upon arriving to the city, they realize that this city is Colossal, with perhaps millions of inhabitants.
    The party is forced to consider another way, as killing millions of innocents really wasn't in the job description. They come up with a plan, but must find jobs, and gather information before they can make any hardline decisiions.

    Papa Charles was kind enough to give Ookla a job as a bouncer as well as a room in the tavern. Ookla's size and strength sure does make him fit. Upon entering the Tavern, Zohe walked up to the shadiest looking person in the place and asked for work to make money so she may buy a clockwork puppy. This guy was...not so impressed by her skills, or lack thereof. He however decided to accept her help anyways, or potentially sell her body for parts, it is unclear yet. Seran followed Zohe in meeting this man and will also potentialy be sold off to the highest bidder.

    Ignum and Stadler are going to go along to the Office of Exchange in an attempt to get a job. And that was where the session ended.

    Last Session

    After having defeated Krampus as per Matty's request, the group heads over to Argona. This is the final task the group has to complete in order to be free of Matty's debt. This city is a floating city that flies way above the clouds, held up by four large propellers. This city used to worship Gond, Matty's deity, but has since turned to worship (Evil snow bitch). In addition to worshipping (evil snow bitch) the older population is quite racist, howver the younger generation is trying to turn around. Due to the people in this city being racist and not worshiping Gond, Matty requested we drop the city and kill everyone in it. Our group had several issues with this task.

    • We do not fully trust Matty, so how can we take his word as true?
    • Just because a city has some racist people, does that justify the murders?
    • Why should we drop a city due to their religion?

    After we made our way up the city our first task was to find out some information. We made our way through Skald Park towards the railway, where we spotted some guards. We knew after spotting them we didn't want to draw attention to ourselves. We took the railway to Leighlines before making our way to Cabriole. There we stumbled across a tavern with the most groovy owner, Papa Charles.
