    Last Session

    After having defeated Krampus as per Matty's request, the group heads over to (NAME OF CITY). This is the final task the group has to complete in order to be free of Matty's debt. This city is a floating city that flies way above the clouds, held up by four large propellers. This city used to worship Gond, Matty's deity, but has since turned to worship (Evil snow bitch). In addition to worshipping (evil snow bitch) the older population is quite racist, howver the younger generation is trying to turn around. Due to the people in this city being racist and not worshiping Gond, Matty requested we drop the city and kill everyone in it. Our group had several issues with this task.

    • We do not fully trust Matty, so how can we take his word as true?
    • Just because a city has some racist people, does that justify the murders?
    • Why should we drop a city due to their religion?

    After we made our way up the city our first task was to find out some information. We made our way through Skald Park towards the railway, where we spotted some guards. We knew after spotting them we didn't want to draw attention to ourselves. We took the railway to Leighlines before making our way to Cabriole. There we stumbles across a tavern with the most groovy owner, Papa Charles.
