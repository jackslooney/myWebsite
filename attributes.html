    • Krampus

    This half-goat half-demon monster punishes those who have misbehaved. He was located in the tavern in a cavern and was found keeping Gond hostage in a large tank.


    Matty the Artificer

    While technically not a monster, his actions definitely take the looks of them. Experimenting on anyone he can get away with, Matty lives in an underground lair, full of the tools of his craft. His moral compass is severely impacted, as he does the very things that he scorns the city of Argona for.


    Yeti

    Large white furred creature that lives in the mountains of Icewind Dale. These creatures are incredibly strong and are capable of hiding in plain sight due to their white fur.


    Owlbear

    A dangerous cross between an owl and a bear. Has the combined abilities of both animals, and is incredibly tacious.


    Hammerhead Salmon

    Large fish native to Icewind Dale. They have developed large heads in order to crack the thick ice that forms on the surface of the lakes.


    Granny

    The incarnation of a powerful fire demon that appeared through the possession of our party members grandmother.


    Monsters

    Throughout our adventures, our group has encountered a multitude of trials. There have been a lot, and we mean a lot of creatures deadset on...well making sure we are dead. So please, enjoy this mini gallery of a few of the creatures we have encountered during our journey.

    Tooth Fairy

    These little demons are able to chomp, induce cavities, and turn invisible. Not very strong but pesky in numbers.


    Nutcracker

    Chompy little buggers that try to end your life.


    Wendigo

    Encountered multiple times by our group. This creature has the ability to mimic sounds and voices.


    Chwinga

    These little forest spirits are mischievous, and quick. They are intelligent, and have their own culture.


    Cragcat

    Icewind Dale's version of a snow leopard. They generally hunt in packs and use their sharp claws and pointy teeth to take down unwary travelers.


    Deer

    A creature that consists of a levitating deer skull. It only has one eye in the center of from which it can shoot beams of light.
